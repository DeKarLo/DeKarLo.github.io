KHAVITS

  • 6 table spoons of flour
  • 50g of butter
  • 3 table spoons of sugar
  • 3 cups of hot water
  • cinnamon
  1. Roast the flour in a dry skillet until it turns a light brown color, stirring constantly.
  2. Add oil while continuing to stir and roast with the oil for an additional 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in boiling water, adding enough to achieve a consistency that is neither too thick nor too thin. Stir until you get a uniform mixture.
  4. Continue to stir until the mixture thickens.
  5. Add sugar and serve, sprinkling with cinnamon and cloves.
